Group 10 ontology for grounding baseline AI Capstone task objects and inferring graspable objects from grasping affordances.

IRI: https://hcis.io/ontology/aicapstone/2026/GraspableObject

A physical object inferred to afford grasping by the robot gripper in the task context.
